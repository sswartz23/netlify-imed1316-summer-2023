About Us

Empowered Vibes Chokers & Jewelry

At Empowered Vibes Jewelry what you see is exactly what you get. Our company is mainly about selling chokers for all people. Having said that we also provide other jewelry accessories. The main goal for us is for each customer to feel welcome and to find the right style, size and the right accessory of his or her choice. If your not quite sure what your style is there is plenty of options to explore on our homepage and in our gallery section. With that said happy shopping!

The choker you see above is a handcrafted leather with a silver cross with just a touch of bling. If you like your chokers with more edge but still classy with a touch of siler look no further this one is for you.

This choker is not just limited to just silver. It also comes in rose gold, gold, bronze, and darken silver. Another option when ordering this item is ordering it as a gift for someone special or to treat yourself instead.
